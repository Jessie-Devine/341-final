Plants are grown for agriculture anywhere humans live permanently! Agriculture is one of the staples of human life in the modern world. "Big Ag" agricultural companies have some very problematic practices that threaten the climate and environment on the Earth. However, individuals participate in agriculture as well, growing plants to eat in gardens and smaller, local farms.

Navigate to the info page to learn about some of the plants people grow to eat, or head over to the gallery to view some pictures.
