The ocean biome is located in the blue areas on the map on the left. It covers 70% of the Earth's surface! Oceans are very important as they regulate weather patterns for the entire planet.

Many plants live here, and in other underwater habitats like swamps, rivers, lakes, and ponds as well. Aquatic plants are vital for the survival of all underwater ecosystems.
