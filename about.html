Plants are typically multicellular and photosynthetic, which means they create their own food. In order to do that, their cells contain chloroplasts. A chloroplast is an organelle which uses water, sunlight, and oxygen to create sugar for the plant to use. They contain chlorophyll, which is why plants are green.
